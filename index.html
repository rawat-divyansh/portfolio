About Me

Defending the Digital World

Hi, I'm Divyansh — a Computer Science student with a clear, focused path into cybersecurity. My immediate goal is to land my first role as a SOC Analyst, working in real-time threat detection, log analysis, and incident response. Long-term, I'm building towards Cloud Security.

I sharpen my skills daily on TryHackMe and Hack The Box — from network forensics and SIEM analysis to web exploitation and malware triage. I believe the best defenders think like attackers.

When I'm not in a terminal, I'm writing CTF write-ups, building security tools, or going deep on a new attack technique. Cybersecurity is not just a career path for me — it's what I actually enjoy.
